🏛 About SPA

SPA (Stable Portfolio Agent) is an automated DeFi yield optimizer running a verified paper track since 2026-06-10. The system allocates $100,000 virtual USDC across audited Ethereum DeFi protocols using a deterministic, LLM-free risk policy (RiskPolicy v1.0). All decisions are logged and auditable.

This track graduates to live capital management after 30 verified days under ADR-002. Go-Live target: 2026-08-01.
