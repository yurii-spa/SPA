How it works

A deterministic daily cycle — no LLM in the loop

Every day the system reads live yields, ranks strategies, and rebalances a virtual portfolio through a rules-only risk gate. Reproducible and logged end-to-end.

01

Read live data

Read-only adapters pull real APY/TVL from whitelisted protocols via DeFiLlama. No writes, no execution.

02

Rank & allocate

Strategies compete in a tournament; the allocator builds a target mix with per-protocol and tier caps.

03

Gate & rebalance

A deterministic RiskPolicy (v1.0) approves or blocks. Approved targets rebalance the paper portfolio; yield accrues.

adapters→tournament→allocator→RiskPolicy gate→rebalance→equity accrual→GoLiveChecker
