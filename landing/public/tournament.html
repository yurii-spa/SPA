📋 Methodology
Backtest Framework
60 strategies evaluated over a 4-year daily simulation spanning 2022-01-01 to 2025-12-31. Each strategy starts with $100,000 USDC. APY data sourced from DeFiLlama historical yields for whitelisted protocols (Aave V3, Compound V3, Morpho, Yearn, Euler V2, Maple).
Noise Model
Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process with seed=42, mean-reversion parameter ρ=0.85. This models realistic APY fluctuations around DeFiLlama historical means without purely deterministic paths. Prevents look-ahead bias.
Ranking Metric
Primary rank: Sharpe ratio (excess return / daily volatility). Secondary: Sortino, Calmar. 100% win rate across all strategies reflects the stablecoin yield nature — no directional risk generating negative return days.
Promotion Policy (ADR-023)
Top-5 enter a 30-day shadow paper phase — strategies simulate allocations alongside the live portfolio without modifying real trades or equity curves. Promotion to live requires passing all 29 GoLive criteria including 30-day paper continuity and gap-monitor check.
⚠️ Advisory only. Backtested results do not guarantee future performance. All results are simulated on historical data. The deterministic RiskPolicy gate (LLM_FORBIDDEN) makes all actual allocation decisions — tournament ranking is advisory input only. Virtual capital $100,000 USDC; no real funds are at risk during the paper trading phase. Strategies classified as T3 or using experimental protocols are excluded from live promotion.
