Architectural planes

Risk /api/tier1/gate

Deterministic backtest→paper gate: real-data, net-of-cost, OOS hold-out, fits a risk package, capacity ≥ capital. approved=False is final.

Execution /api/execution/readiness

Paper-safe posture: adapters dry-run, kill-switch readable, no signer in env. Gated until custody + track record are met.

Audit / Provenance /api/tier1/nav

Verifiable NAV proof: positions → computed NAV reconciled against reported equity within tolerance, with canonical component & NAV hashes.

Governance /api/governance

Default-DENY policy table (AUTO / HUMAN_SINGLE / HUMAN_DUAL). Fund movement & keys require dual control.

Observability /api/live/agents

launchd fleet heartbeat — every agent's status, freshness and exit code, plus system health checks.

Self-Healing /api/live/agents

Deterministic recovery: self_heal revives dead agents & recovers missed cycles; threat_reactor auto-arms the kill-switch on critical threats. Both every 5 min.

Strategy / Promotion /api/tier1/packages

Validated strategies grouped into risk packages, advanced through a one-way promotion pipeline.
