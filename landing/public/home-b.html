Field Notes

Why paper first, and how the engine decides

Most yield products lead with a number. This one leads with a method. Before any real capital, the system has to prove — on live data, in the open — that its decisions are reproducible, conservative, and dull in exactly the right way.

What this is

A virtual $100,000 USDC portfolio, run for real

Each day pulls live APY and TVL from a whitelist of read-only DeFi adapters — Aave V3, Compound V3, Morpho, Yearn, and more — and rebalances a virtual book. The capital is paper; the data, the logic, and the discipline are not.

How it decides

Deterministic. No LLM in the risk path.

A fixed RiskPolicy gates every allocation: TVL ≥ $5M per pool, per-protocol caps (40% Tier-1 / 20% Tier-2), APY bounded 1–30%, a 5% cash buffer, and a drawdown kill-switch. The same inputs always produce the same decision — and a rejection cannot be overridden.

What we log

Every trade, gate, and equity tick is public

The dashboard streams the live portfolio, the daily cycle, every risk-gate outcome, and the equity curve straight from the production host. Nothing is curated after the fact.

When go-live

30 honest days, then own capital only

Go-live needs all 29 checklist criteria passing for 7 consecutive days plus a manual owner review. Even then, the first live phase uses own capital only. No third-party funds at this stage.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this a real trading system?▾
Real APY/TVL data from live DeFi protocols, a real deterministic risk policy, real daily cycles — but no real capital yet. It is a paper simulation on a virtual $100,000 USDC portfolio, running since June 10, 2026.
How are protocols selected?▾
Whitelisted protocols only — Tier-1 (Aave V3, Compound V3, Morpho) and Tier-2. RiskPolicy v1.0 enforces TVL ≥ $5M per pool, per-protocol caps (40% T1 / 20% T2), T2 total ≤ 50%, an APY gate of 1–30%, and a 5% cash buffer.
Are you raising or holding capital?▾
No. There are no deposits, withdrawals, or onboarding. This is a research project building a public record. The first live phase, if it happens, will use own capital only.
When does live trading start?▾
Only after all 29 go-live criteria pass for 7 consecutive days plus a manual owner review. Target is roughly mid-2026 — contingent, not promised.
