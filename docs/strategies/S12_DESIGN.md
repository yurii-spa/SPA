## Kill-Switch Logic

`BaseGasMonitor` (ADR-025) tracks Base chain gas price daily:
- **Normal:** Base gas 0.001–0.1 Gwei → Phase 2 active
- **Kill-switch trigger:** Gas > 10 Gwei for 3+ consecutive days
- **Kill-switch action:** S12 reverts to Phase 1 ETH fallback weights (0% Base)
- **Recovery:** Automatic when `consecutive_above < 3` (monitoring continues)
