# 42 — External Capital Readiness (§30)

**Status: STUB.** This document is a Priority-3 placeholder for the readiness checklist that must be
satisfied **before** any external (non-owner) capital is accepted. It lists the checklist items only;
per-item requirements are deferred.

**Scope discipline — hard gate.** **No external capital is accepted without legal review** (see
`06_spa_core_invariants.md`, invariant E-18). External capital is out of scope until this checklist
is fully satisfied and owner-approved. Nothing in the research layer accepts, custodies, or moves
external funds.

**Cross-references:** `docs/41_performance_reporting_methodology.md` (track/reporting methodology),
`docs/39_investment_committee_workflow.md` (allocation governance), `docs/45_compliance_map.md`
(compliance surface).

## Planned contents (outline only)

- **Legal review** — mandatory prerequisite; no external capital without it.
- **Entity / structure** — legal entity, fund structuring, jurisdiction of formation.
- **Disclosures** — risk disclosures, offering documents, no-guarantee framing.
- **Custody** — custody model (non-custodial / qualified custodian); key-handling boundaries
  (invariant B — no private keys / signing in-system).
- **Reporting & track methodology** — evidenced-track standard, paper-vs-live honesty, cadence
  (cross-ref `docs/41`).
- **KYC / AML** — investor onboarding, screening, record-keeping.
- **Jurisdiction** — permitted investor jurisdictions; regulatory constraints per region.
- **Readiness gate** — the full checklist must pass + owner + legal sign-off before any acceptance.
