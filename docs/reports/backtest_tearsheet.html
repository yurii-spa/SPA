▶ Caveats & Disclosures

Backtest is not a guarantee of future performance. Past results in simulated environments do not predict future returns.

Data source: DeFiLlama historical APY/TVL data. APY figures are historical, not guaranteed. Backtests use closing APY snapshots; live yield may differ due to timing and gas.

Execution risk not modelled: No slippage, no smart contract risk, no liquidity risk, no gas costs in backtest. T-bill proxy is regime-adjusted (4% annualised), not actual T-bill rates.

Structural properties: Stablecoin yield strategies have near-zero price volatility, resulting in structurally high Sharpe/Sortino ratios and ~0% drawdown. This is a property of monotonic yield accrual, not an artefact.
